Fireplaces

Custom Fireplace Design & Installation

A fireplace is more than a heat source — it's a centerpiece for your living space. Whether you want a classic wood-burning fireplace, a modern gas insert, or an elegant electric unit, we design and install fireplaces that suit your style and home. We also build custom mantels, surrounds, and outdoor fire features.

Our team handles everything from framing and venting to finishing with stone, tile, brick, or wood. We ensure your fireplace is safe, efficient, and visually stunning.

  • Gas fireplace installation
  • Wood-burning fireplaces
  • Electric fireplace inserts
  • Custom mantels & surrounds
  • Outdoor fire pits & fireplaces
  • Stone, brick & tile finishing
