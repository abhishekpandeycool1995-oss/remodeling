About Us

Your Trusted Remodeling Partner in Texas

Texas Home & Remodel was founded with a simple mission: deliver exceptional craftsmanship and honest, transparent service to every client. Based in Hockley, TX and serving the Greater Houston area, we specialize in transforming houses into homes that reflect your unique style and needs.

As a BBB-accredited business with $2,000,000 in liability coverage and a 2-year workmanship warranty, we provide complete peace of mind on every project — from small repairs to full custom home builds.
